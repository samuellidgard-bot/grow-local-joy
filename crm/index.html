Writing rules

Message Guardrails

  • Write like Sam: clear, friendly, direct and local.
  • First touch = observation, compliment, simple question.
  • Do not explain the full service before they reply.
  • Use a 10-minute chat only as a soft next step, not a hard pitch.
  • Lead with the starter foundations offer first. Only move into the 14-day lead test when the basics are ready.
  • Do not mention the £1,500/month retainer in cold outreach.
  • Avoid fake case studies, guaranteed results and corporate fluff.
  • Draft only. Nothing sends without human approval.
