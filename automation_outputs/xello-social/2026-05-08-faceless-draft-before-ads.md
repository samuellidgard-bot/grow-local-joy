# 2026-05-08 Faceless Draft: Before Ads, Fix This First

Pillar: Marketing foundations

Audience: Local service businesses, trades, renovation companies and small business owners who want better enquiries from social media, websites and AI-supported marketing.

## Core Lesson

Paid ads work better when the basics are ready. If the offer, proof, enquiry route and tracking are weak, more traffic usually creates more confusion instead of better enquiries.
