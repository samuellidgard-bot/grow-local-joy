## Best Pattern Found

Prompt reveal and framework carousels are strong for Xello because they give immediate utility and are naturally saveable.

## Why The Pattern Works

The audience gets a reusable asset, not just advice. A prompt/template also gives a clear reason to save the post and follow for more.

## Xello Application

Turn the 30-day sprint into repeatable prompt drops:

- AI content prompt.
- Instagram bio rewrite prompt.
- Website CTA audit prompt.
- Reel hook generator prompt.
- Lead-quality checklist prompt.

## Tomorrow's Content Instruction

When drafting the next post, make the first frame specific and action-led. Prefer:

```text
Steal this AI prompt for 30 days of content
```

over:

```text
AI content planning tips
```

## Design Lesson

One main idea per slide. Avoid packing multiple mini-paragraphs into a single carousel slide.

## Hook Lesson

Use a direct benefit or warning in the first 1-2 seconds:

- "Stop..."
- "Steal this..."
- "Before..."
- "Most businesses miss..."

## CTA Lesson

Prompt posts should use:

```text
Comment PROMPT + save for later.
```

At this stage Sam should reply manually or pin the prompt, not automate DMs.

## One Thing To Avoid

Do not use fake performance statistics on dashboard-style posts. Use qualitative metrics unless there is real Xello data.
