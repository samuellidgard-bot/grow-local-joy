# Canva Template Mapping Guide

Use this to adapt any strong Canva template to the Xello carousels.

## Slide Type Mapping

### Title Slide

Use for:

- Carousel title.
- One short subline.
- Xello logo or small handle.

Do not overload this slide with service lists.

### Problem Slide

Use for:

- "More traffic is not the first fix."
- "Likes are not leads."
- "A weak profile creates doubt."

Visual treatment:

- One icon.
- One bold headline.
- One short body sentence.

### Checklist Slide

Use for:

- Foundations list.
- Starter offer parts.
- Google Business profile checks.

Visual treatment:

- 3-6 compact cards.
- Green ticks.
- Minimal text.

### Process Slide

Use for:

- Footage to content.
- Ad click to enquiry.
- Profile proof to trust.

Visual treatment:

- Arrows.
- Simple pipeline.
- 3-4 steps maximum.

### CTA Slide

Use for:

- Follow @xello_media.
- Visit xellomedia.co.uk.
- Contact line.

Visual treatment:

- Strong headline.
- Contact details smaller.
- No clutter.

## Xello Color Conversion

If the Canva template has:

- White background -> change to black or graphite.
- Blue/purple accent -> change to lime green.
- Pastel card -> change to dark graphite with green border or solid lime card.
- Black text on light background -> use white or black depending on the new card color.

Suggested colors:

- Background: `#050705`
- Graphite: `#202020`
- Dark card: `#151915`
- Lime green: `#7BC438`
- Bright accent: `#9DFF3A`
- White: `#F4F7F0`
- Muted silver: `#BFC4BA`

## Font Guidance

Use bold condensed or heavy sans-serif for headlines.

Good Canva font directions:

- League Spartan
- Anton
- Bebas Neue
- Montserrat ExtraBold
- Archivo Black
- Oswald

Use simple sans-serif for body text:

- Montserrat
- Inter
- Open Sans
- Manrope
