## Caption

Your website does not need to be complicated.

But it does need to answer a few things quickly:

- what do you do?
- where do you work?
- what kind of jobs are right for you?
- why should someone trust you?
- how do they enquire?

That is why Xello checks the enquiry route before scaling content or ads.
