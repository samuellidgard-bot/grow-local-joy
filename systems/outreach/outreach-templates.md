# Xello Outreach Templates

These templates are the baseline tone for the AI agent. Keep them specific, polite and short.

## Email 1

Subject: Quick growth audit for {{company_name}}

Hi {{first_name_or_team}},

I came across {{company_name}} while looking at renovation companies around {{area}}.

You already seem to handle {{service_type}} work, but there may be room to turn more local homeowners into quote enquiries through a stronger website journey, Meta ads and follow-up process.

Xello Media manages this end to end for renovation companies: website improvements, Meta ads, social growth and lead tracking.

Would you like me to send over a short growth audit with a few practical opportunities?

Sam  
Xello Media  

If this is not relevant, no problem. Reply and I will not follow up.
