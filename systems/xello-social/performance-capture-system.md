# Xello Social Performance Capture System

Created: 2026-05-08

Purpose: teach the agents from Xello's actual account results.

The Growth Scout learns from the wider market. This file makes the system learn from Xello's own posts.

## When To Capture Results

For every post, record results at:

- 24 hours.
- 72 hours.
- 7 days if the post keeps moving.

## Metrics To Record

Required:

- Date posted.
- Platform.
- Format.
- Pillar.
- Hook.
- Views.
- Saves.
- Shares.
- Comments.
- Profile visits.
- Follows gained.
- DMs/enquiries.
- Live link.

Optional:

- Average watch time.
- Reel completion rate.
- Swipe-through rate.
- Website clicks.
- Best comment/question.

## How To Interpret Results

### Reels

Primary signals:

- Watch time.
- Replays.
- Shares.
- Follows gained.

If views are high but follows are low, the hook may be too broad or the profile promise may be weak.

### Carousels

Primary signals:

- Saves.
- Shares.
- Swipe-through.
- Profile visits.

If saves are high but follows are low, the final slide and caption CTA may need a stronger reason to follow.

### LinkedIn Repurposes

Primary signals:

- Comments.
- Profile visits.
- Connection requests.
- DMs.

If impressions are low but comments are high quality, keep the topic because it may attract business owners.

## Learning Labels

Add one label to each measured post:

- `Repeat`: make a similar post soon.
- `Variant`: keep the topic but change hook/format.
- `Pause`: weak result, do not repeat immediately.
- `Upgrade`: strong idea but execution needs better design/copy.
- `Convert`: good post for turning into a Reel, carousel or lead magnet.

## Weekly Review Rule

Every weekly review should choose:

- 1 post to repeat.
- 1 post to turn into another format.
- 1 weak pattern to stop.
- 1 hook type to test next week.
- 1 design improvement.
