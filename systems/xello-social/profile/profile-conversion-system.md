# Xello Instagram Profile Conversion System

Created: 2026-05-08

Purpose: make profile visits convert into followers, DMs and link clicks.

The content gets people to the profile. The profile must make them understand why to follow.

## Current Account

Instagram:

`@xello_media`

Positioning:

```text
AI + social media systems for small businesses
Marketing tips, growth hacks + prompt drops
Turn attention into qualified enquiries
UK based
```

## Profile Conversion Checklist

### Bio

Must answer:

- What does Xello teach?
- Who is it for?
- What result does it help with?
- Why follow now?

### Profile Photo

Use:

- Xello X mark.
- High contrast.
- No tiny text.
- Premium black/green/white.

### Link In Bio

Phase 1:

- Website or simple waitlist/free prompt pack.

Phase 2:

- Free prompt pack landing page.

Phase 3:

- Lead magnet plus service CTA.

### Pinned Posts

Pin these three:

1. Start Here: What Xello Growth Lab Teaches.
2. Free AI Prompts For Small Business Content.
3. Views Don't Pay You. Enquiries Do.

### Highlights

Once stories begin, use:

- Start Here
- Free Prompts
- AI Tools
- Growth Tips
- Results

## Weekly Metrics

Track:

- Profile visits.
- Follows gained.
- Follow rate: `follows gained / profile visits`.
- Link clicks.
- DMs.
- Most common audience question.

## Interpretation

If posts get views but profile visits are low:

- Hooks/topics are too broad or not connected to Xello.

If profile visits are high but follows are low:

- Bio, pinned posts or profile promise need improving.

If follows are growing but DMs are low:

- Add stronger prompt/lead magnet CTAs.

If DMs are low quality:

- Add more content about business fit, local service businesses and better enquiries.
