# Canva Template Spec: Checklist / System

Created: 2026-05-08

Use for posts such as:

- "Stop running ads until this is fixed"
- "Fix your Instagram profile in 10 minutes"
- "The no-budget marketing system for local businesses"

## Format

- Instagram carousel: 1080x1350 preferred, or 1080x1080 if keeping square.
- Reel version can use the same checklist as animated text.

## Visual System

- Background: black/graphite.
- Accent: Xello green warning/check line.
- Main element: large checklist ticks.
- Small slide counter top right.

## Slide Structure

1. Big warning hook:
   `STOP RUNNING ADS UNTIL THIS IS FIXED ->`
2. Question:
   `Can a stranger understand your offer in 5 seconds?`
3. Question:
   `Do you show real proof, not just claims?`
4. Question:
   `Is there one obvious enquiry button?`
5. Question:
   `Are you tracking where every lead came from?`
6. CTA:
   `Traffic won't fix weak foundations. SAVE THIS CHECKLIST.`

## Rules

- Each slide should feel like a quick audit question.
- Do not turn the checklist into a paragraph.
- Use final slide to make the philosophy memorable.
