# Canva Template Spec: Myth Busting

Created: 2026-05-08

Use for posts such as:

- "Views don't pay you. Enquiries do."
- "AI won't fix bad marketing. It will speed it up."
- "More leads is not always better."

## Format

- Instagram carousel: 1080x1350 preferred, or 1080x1080 if keeping square.

## Visual System

- Background: dark graphite.
- Accent: Xello green.
- Main element: dashboard-style cards or myth/truth badges.
- No fake numeric stats unless real.

## Slide Structure

1. Big contrarian hook:
   `VIEWS DON'T PAY YOU. ENQUIRIES DO. ->`
2. Reframe:
   `Saves = intent`
3. Reframe:
   `Shares = trust`
4. Reframe:
   `Comments = conversation`
5. Reframe:
   `DMs = opportunity`
6. CTA:
   `Stop chasing views. Build content that starts better conversations. SAVE THIS.`

## Rules

- Use qualitative metrics, not fake percentages.
- One reframe per slide.
- Keep final slide strong enough to share.
