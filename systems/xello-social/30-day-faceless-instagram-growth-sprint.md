## Faceless Content System

### Format 1: Text-Over-Motion Reel

Use for:

- Hook lists.
- Mistakes.
- Growth hacks.
- "Do this, not this."

Structure:

- 0-2s: sharp claim.
- 2-12s: 3-5 quick points.
- 12-15s: save/follow CTA.

Visual:

- Xello green and black motion graphics.
- Big readable text.
- Subtle progress bar.

### Format 2: Screen Recording Tutorial

Use for:

- AI prompts.
- Canva setup.
- CRM/content planning.
- Caption drafting.
- Website or profile audits.

Structure:

- Problem.
- Show the exact prompt/tool.
- Show the result.
- Give one warning.

### Format 3: Prompt Giveaway Reel

Use for:

- Comment-trigger content.
- Saves and shares.
- Follower conversion.

Structure:

- "Steal this prompt..."
- Show the use case.
- Show the prompt framework.
- CTA: "Comment PROMPT and I will send/post the template."

Manual note: do not automate DMs at first. Reply manually or post the prompt in the comments/caption.

### Format 4: Premium Carousel

Use for:

- Authority.
- Saveable frameworks.
- Step-by-step systems.
- "30-second MBA" style lessons.

Structure:

- Slide 1: painful hook.
- Slides 2-6: one idea per slide.
- Final slide: save/follow CTA.

### Format 5: AI News Translated For Business Owners

Use for:

- Timely AI/social news.
- Platform changes.
- Tool updates.

Structure:

- "New AI/social update..."
- "What it actually means..."
- "How a small business can use it..."
- "What to avoid..."

Rule: never just report news. Translate it into a useful business action.

## 4-Hour Weekly Automation Workflow

Goal: batch 7 posts per week.

### Hour 1: Research And Topic Selection

Tools:

- ChatGPT/Codex for ideas and angle selection.
- Instagram/TikTok manual trend scan.
- Saved comments/questions.
- Xello CRM notes.

Output:

- 7 post topics.
- 7 hooks.
- 1 prompt giveaway.
- 1 carousel authority post.

### Hour 2: Script And Caption Generation

Tools:

- ChatGPT/Codex.
- Xello faceless content machine.

Output for each post:

- 15-second script.
- On-screen text.
- Caption.
- CTA.
- Hashtags.
- Safety check.

### Hour 3: Visual Production

Tools:

- Canva for carousels and static graphics.
- CapCut for text-led Reels.
- Sora or similar AI video generation only for abstract backgrounds, not fake client proof.
- Screen recording for AI prompts, CRM workflows and website examples.

Rules:

- Use original visuals where possible.
- Avoid reposting other creators' content.
- No watermarks.
- No fake client results.
- Keep text readable on mobile.

### Hour 4: Edit, QA And Schedule Manually

Tools:

- CapCut.
- Canva.
- Instagram draft/scheduler or approved scheduling tool.

Checklist:

- First frame is clear.
- The post makes one point.
- CTA is simple.
- No private client info.
- No unsupported claims.
- Save/share reason is obvious.
- Caption includes relevant keywords.

## Premium Visual Rules

- Big text, few words.
- Black/white/grey with Xello green highlights.
- Use the X logo as a small corner bug.
- Avoid cluttered templates.
- Use motion sparingly.
- Make carousels feel like useful mini-guides.

## Growth Rules

- Reels grow reach.
- Carousels build authority and saves.
- Prompt giveaways create comments and follows.
- LinkedIn reposts can attract business owners.
- Reply to every useful comment manually.
- Track which topics produce saves, shares, profile visits and DMs.

## What Not To Do

- Do not chase random viral AI facts with no link to Xello.
- Do not claim guaranteed follower or lead results.
- Do not pretend AI content is human-made if disclosure is required.
- Do not repost other creators' content.
- Do not use fake screenshots, fake testimonials or fake earnings.
- Do not make every post a sales pitch.
