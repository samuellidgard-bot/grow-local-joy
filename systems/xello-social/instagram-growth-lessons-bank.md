## Active Rules For Tomorrow's Drafts

1. Lead with pain or curiosity in the first frame.
   - Use: "Stop...", "Before...", "Most businesses miss...", "Steal this..."
   - Avoid: vague educational titles with no tension.

2. Make every carousel slide one clear aha.
   - One point per slide.
   - Big text.
   - No pitch-deck paragraphs.

3. Translate AI/social news into a business action.
   - Do not just report updates.
   - Always answer: "What should a small business do with this?"

4. Prefer save/share mechanics over vanity likes.
   - Checklists, prompts, templates and frameworks should be designed to save.
   - Myth-busting posts should be designed to share.

5. Keep Xello original.
   - Learn formats and patterns.
   - Do not copy wording, layouts or other creators' exact assets.

6. Tie follower-growth content back to better enquiries.
   - AI and social media hacks should eventually connect to trust, lead quality, content systems, website clarity or business outcomes.

## Hook Patterns To Test

- "Stop [common action] until [foundation] is fixed."
- "Steal this [specific prompt/template] for [specific result]."
- "Most [business type] are losing [result] because of this."
- "Views do not pay you. [Business outcome] does."
- "If I had to grow [business type] from zero, I would post this."
- "Before you post again, check this."
- "AI will not fix [bad thing]. It will speed it up."

## Design Lessons

- First slide text should be 20-30% larger than instinct.
- Use one visual metaphor per carousel: checklist, prompt card, dashboard, mistake/fix or myth/truth.
- Keep the Xello logo small. The lesson is the hero.
- Use green as a signal, not a full background.
- Avoid made-up stats. Use qualitative labels unless real data exists.

## CTA Lessons

Use CTAs matched to post type:

- Checklist: "Save this before you run ads."
- Prompt: "Comment PROMPT and save for later."
- Myth-busting: "Share this with someone chasing views."
- Framework: "Save this system."
- Build-in-public: "Follow to watch the system being built."
