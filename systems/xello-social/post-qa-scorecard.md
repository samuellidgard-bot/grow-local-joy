# Xello Social Post QA Scorecard

Created: 2026-05-08

Use this before Sam manually posts any Xello faceless Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn content.

Minimum publish score: `45/60`

If a draft scores under `45/60`, improve it before posting.

## Scorecard

| Area | Points | What Good Looks Like |
|---|---:|---|
| Hook strength | 10 | The first frame creates pain, curiosity or immediate utility in 1-2 seconds. |
| Save value | 10 | The viewer has a clear reason to save: checklist, prompt, template, framework or repeatable system. |
| Share value | 10 | The post expresses something a business owner would send to a friend, partner or team member. |
| Visual clarity | 10 | Mobile-readable, one idea per slide/frame, strong contrast, no clutter. |
| Niche fit | 10 | Clearly fits AI, social media growth, marketing systems, prompts or better enquiries. |
| Follower conversion | 10 | Gives a reason to follow Xello for more, not just like one post. |

## Score Bands

- `55-60`: Excellent. Publish manually if safety checklist passes.
- `45-54`: Good. Publish manually after small wording/design polish.
- `35-44`: Needs revision. Improve the weak category before posting.
- Under `35`: Do not post. Rewrite the angle.

## QA Questions

Before publishing, answer:

- Does the first slide/frame stop the scroll?
- Could a viewer understand the point without reading the caption?
- Is there one clear action: save, share, comment or follow?
- Is the content original to Xello's angle?
- Is it useful to a small business owner, not only interesting to marketers?
- Are there any unsupported claims, fake results or private client details?
