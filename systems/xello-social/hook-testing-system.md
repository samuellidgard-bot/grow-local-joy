# Xello Hook Testing System

Created: 2026-05-08

Purpose: improve reach by making every draft test multiple hook angles before Sam posts.

Every automated post draft must include three hook options.

## Hook Types

### 1. Safe / Clear Hook

Use when the topic needs immediate clarity.

Examples:

- `5 things to fix before running ads`
- `A simple AI prompt for small business content`
- `How to turn one service into 10 post ideas`

### 2. Strong / Pain Hook

Use when the account needs stronger scroll-stopping energy.

Examples:

- `Stop running ads until this is fixed`
- `Your content gets views but no enquiries because of this`
- `AI will not fix bad marketing. It will speed it up.`

### 3. Viral / Curiosity Hook

Use when the topic needs more intrigue.

Examples:

- `This is why small business content feels invisible`
- `The prompt I would use if I had 0 content ideas`
- `Most businesses are tracking the wrong number`

## Choosing The Winner

Pick the hook that best matches the post goal:

- Reach: curiosity or pain.
- Saves: clear utility.
- Shares: contrarian or myth-busting.
- Follows: recurring promise or strong POV.
- Enquiries: specific business outcome.

## Required Draft Section

Every draft should include:

```text
Hook options:
1. Safe/clear:
2. Strong/pain:
3. Viral/curiosity:

Chosen hook:
Reason:
```

## Hook Rules

- First 6 words must carry the point.
- Avoid clever wording that hides the benefit.
- Use the audience's language: business, content, leads, enquiries, ads, AI, social media.
- Do not overpromise results.
- Avoid generic hooks such as "3 tips for..."
- Use "you" or "your business" where possible.

## Weekly Review Rule

The weekly growth review should identify:

- Best hook type.
- Worst hook type.
- One hook format to repeat next week.
- One hook format to stop using.
