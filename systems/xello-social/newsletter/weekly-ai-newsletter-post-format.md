# Weekly AI Newsletter Post Format

Created: 2026-05-08

Purpose: create a weekly recurring Xello content series that translates AI/social media news into practical business moves.

Series name options:

1. `AI Growth Brief`
2. `Xello Weekly AI Brief`
3. `This Week In AI Marketing`

Recommended:

```text
Xello Weekly AI Brief
```

## Positioning

```text
The useful AI and social media updates small businesses should actually care about.
```

## Carousel Format

Template family:

Use a new variant of Checklist/System or Myth Busting.

Slides:

1. `Xello Weekly AI Brief: 3 updates small businesses should know ->`
2. Update 1: what happened + why it matters.
3. Update 2: what happened + why it matters.
4. Update 3: what happened + why it matters.
5. What to do this week.
6. Save/follow CTA.

## Reel Format

Length:

15-25 seconds.

Structure:

```text
Hook: 3 AI updates that matter for small businesses this week.
Update 1: one sentence.
Update 2: one sentence.
Update 3: one sentence.
Action: here is what I would do.
CTA: follow Xello for weekly AI marketing briefs.
```

## LinkedIn Format

Structure:

```text
3 AI/social updates small businesses should care about this week:

1. [Update]
Why it matters:
What to do:

2. [Update]
Why it matters:
What to do:

3. [Update]
Why it matters:
What to do:

The pattern:
[One paragraph about the wider lesson.]
```

## Prompt Giveaway Tie-In

Each newsletter should include one practical prompt, such as:

```text
Act as a marketing assistant for my small business.
Here are 3 AI/social updates from this week:
[PASTE UPDATES]
Turn them into:
1. One Instagram post idea
2. One customer education idea
3. One internal workflow improvement
4. One thing to ignore for now
```

## Quality Rules

- No more than 3 updates.
- Each update must include "what to do".
- Avoid hype words like "game-changing" unless justified.
- Always connect back to small business marketing, AI use, content, enquiries or trust.
- Use source names/links in the draft notes.
